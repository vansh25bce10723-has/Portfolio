✅ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Always verify calls claiming to be from banks
Use only official apps for payments
Enable two-factor authentication
Regularly check your bank statements
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
Immediately report fraud to National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
Call Helpline 1930 in case of financial fraud
