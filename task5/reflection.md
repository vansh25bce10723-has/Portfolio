✍️ Reflection 

While researching about cybercrime, I was surprised to see how easily people fall victim to simple scams like phishing and UPI fraud. Fraudsters use very convincing methods, such as pretending to be bank officials or creating fake websites that look real. I realized that even a small mistake, like clicking on a suspicious link, can lead to serious financial loss.

This made me more aware of the importance of digital safety. One habit I will change is being more cautious before clicking on any unknown links or sharing any personal information online. I will also educate my friends and family about these risks so that they can stay safe. Awareness and careful behavior are the best ways to prevent cybercrime.
