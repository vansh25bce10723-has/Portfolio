🛡️ Case Study: UPI / Online Payment Fraud 

Online payment fraud, especially UPI fraud, has become very common among students and general users in India. In this type of cybercrime, fraudsters trick people into sharing sensitive information like OTPs, UPI PINs, or by sending fake payment links.

For example, a college student received a call from a person pretending to be a bank executive. The caller informed him about a “KYC update issue” and asked him to click on a link sent via SMS. The link led to a fake website that looked exactly like the bank’s official page. The student entered his UPI details and OTP, believing it to be genuine. Within minutes, a large amount of money was deducted from his account.

Such frauds usually target students, elderly people, and those unfamiliar with digital security practices. The consequences include financial loss, mental stress, and loss of trust in online systems.

These scams happen mainly due to lack of awareness and urgency created by fraudsters. Therefore, it is very important to verify sources and never share confidential information online.
